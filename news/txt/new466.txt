Goldie Lookin Chain announce Welsh tour
Goldie Lookin Chain have announced a tour of Wales. The Newport rappers will play a special week of shows in their home country to coincide with the release of their next single ‘R&B’. ‘R&B’ will hit the shops on November 21, and a full UK tour will follow in February 2006. The dates are: Blackwood Miner’s Institute (November 21) Swansea Patti Pavilion (22) Bangor University (23) Aberystwyth University (24) Bridgend Recreation Centre (25) Wrexham Central Station (26) Brecon Market Hall (27) For ticket availability, go to NME Tickets or call them on 0870 1 663 663.
