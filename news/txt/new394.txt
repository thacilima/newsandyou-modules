NME News Desk, 6th July 2016 Related Articles Watch Justin Bieber's acoustic cover of Taylor Swift's 'I Knew You Were Trouble' Watch as Justin Bieber tour continues despite ankle injury Justin Bieber addresses recent stage fall Justin Bieber is set to appear on a new song together with Ed Sheeran, MØ and Benny Blanco. The track, titled ‘Cold Water’, is produced by Diplo’s Major Lazer. It will be the first new collaboration from the pair since the hit single ‘Where Are Ü Now’. MØ has also previously worked with Diplo, featuring on ‘Lean On’. Bieber announced the track on Twitter this Friday, it’s thought to be premiering on Beats 1 later this month. #coldwater coming this month.
