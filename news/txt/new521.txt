Lady Gaga to co-host BBC Radio 1 Breakfast Show tomorrow
Lady Gaga will co-host the Radio 1 Breakfast Show tomorrow (September 9), the BBC’s flagship station has announced. The pop singer will join Nick Grimshaw during his show from 6.30am to 10am to discuss her comeback single ‘Perfect Illusion’ as it drops tomorrow. Gaga tweeted the single’s release date earlier this week alongside a photo of its cover art plus what appear to be lyrics from the song.
