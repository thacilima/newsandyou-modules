Watch ‘Orange Is The New Black”s Natasha Lyonne star in Against Me!’s video for ‘333’
Natasha Lyonne stars in the new video from Against Me!. The Orange Is The New Black actress plays a ’60s housewife who flees the mundanity of marriage via a series of fantasies. The clip for ‘333’ is directed by Better Off Dead actress Ione Skye and also features Bates Motel actor Andrew Howard as Lyonne’s husband. Watch the video below, via Rolling Stone. In a statement, Skye said: “This project attracted a lot of talented people: an incredible director of photography, producer, costume designer, editor and set designer. I felt very supported and inspired.” ‘333’ features on Against Me!’s new album ‘Shape Shift With Me’, which is released on September 16.
