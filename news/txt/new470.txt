Dead 60s set for winter tour
The Dead 60s have confirmed a quartet of UK dates. The Liverpool band who have spent much of the year in America – even delaying the release of their debut album in the UK until the autumn – due to their US popularity, are set to make it up to their British fans with a short winter tour. The band will play: Birmingham Academy 2 (Nov 28) Liverpool Academy (Dec 2) Norwich Waterfront (6) London Electric Ballroom (8) For ticket availability, go to NME Tickets or call them on 0870 1 663 663.
